Module 1: What is a Prompt?

Definition

A prompt is the text you give to an AI to get a response. It's how you communicate your needs, questions, or tasks to the AI. The quality of your prompt directly affects the quality of the AI's response.

Why Prompt Engineering Matters

Poor prompts give poor results. Great prompts unlock the full power of AI. Learning to write effective prompts is the most valuable skill in the AI era.

Good vs Bad Prompts

❌ Bad Prompt:

Write something about dogs.

✅ Good Prompt:

Write a 200-word blog post about golden retrievers as family pets, including their temperament, exercise needs, and why they're great with children. Use a friendly, informative tone.

Key Elements

• Context — Background information
• Specificity — Clear, detailed instructions
• Format — How you want the output
• Tone — The style of response

Module 2: Prompt Patterns

The Role Pattern

Tell the AI who it is. This improves responses dramatically.

"You are an experienced Python tutor. Explain list comprehensions to a beginner programmer."

The Step-by-Step Pattern

Ask the AI to break down complex tasks.

"Explain how to build a website. Break this down into 5 clear steps."

The Example Pattern

Show the AI what you want with examples.

"Write product descriptions like this example: [insert example]. Now write one for a wireless mouse."

The Format Pattern

Specify exactly how you want the output.

"Give me a meal plan in this format: Breakfast: [food], Lunch: [food], Dinner: [food]"

Module 3: Advanced Techniques

Chain of Thought

Ask the AI to think step-by-step. This improves reasoning and accuracy.

"Solve this math problem step-by-step, showing your work: If a train travels 120 km in 2 hours, how far will it travel in 5 hours at the same speed?"

Few-Shot Learning

Give multiple examples for complex tasks.

"Here are 3 examples of friendly customer service responses: [examples]. Now write a response to: 'My order hasn't arrived yet.'"

Constraints & Boundaries

Set clear limits for better results.

"Write a summary in exactly 3 bullet points, each under 10 words. Do not use technical jargon."

Iterative Refinement

Start simple, then improve. Ask the AI to revise based on feedback.

"That's good, but make it more formal and add statistics."
